A Poem For You

In quiet strength, you always stand,

My guiding light, my helping hand.
At times I'm silent, lost in thought,
Yet all you've given can't be bought.

No riches match your daily grace,
Over time, love lines your face.
Through storms and sun, you've always been,

Shielding me from things unseen.
All those lessons, softly said,
You built the dreams I've always fed.

I may not always find the way,
To thank you, Dad, for each new day.

As years go by, my heart still sees,
Love in your silent victories.
Words may falter, yet it's true,
A father's love sees me through.
You gave me wings, you kept me grounded,
So much more love surrounded.

Behind each scolding, a heart that cares,
Underneath, a man with worries which he never shares.
This Father's Day, I hope you see,

I carry your strength inside of me.
Life moves fast, but one thing's true—
One part of me belongs to you.
Voices fade, but yours remains,
Echoing through joys and pains.

You're my hero, tried and true,
On this day, I honor you.
Unspoken words — I love you too.
